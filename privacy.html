Privacy Policy

Effective Date:23th/July/2025

Last Update: 25th/July/2029

At Formosa Fabella Naturae, we value the privacy of our visitors and participants. This policy outlines the nature of the information we collect and how it is used to enhance the symbolic, educational, and immersive experience of our digital environment. We may collect information voluntarily provided by users, such as names, email addresses, and written contributions. Additionally, we track general usage data — including browser types, time spent on pages, and navigational patterns — to better understand our audience and improve site functionality. All collected information is used strictly for improving the platform’s performance, responding to user inquiries, delivering personalized content (like symbolic interpretations or thematic recommendations), and sending occasional updates to those who opt in. We do not collect sensitive personal data unless it is specifically offered for creative or consultation purposes. We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. Information is shared only with service providers who help us maintain our infrastructure or if legally required by applicable regulations. All data is stored using secure, encrypted methods and access is restricted to authorized personnel only. Users have control over their data. You may request access to your personal information, ask for its deletion, or opt out of communications. Browser-level controls and cookie preferences can be adjusted to limit passive tracking as well.
